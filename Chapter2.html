Chapter 2

A big, ripe pumpkin from the vegetable patch grew so big it looked as if it was ready to pop!

The enormous pumpkin transformed into a beautiful carriage, while four mice became stunning white horses! As the magic swirled around, a brown rat turned into a coachman and Cinderella clapped her hands.

With a clap of her hands, Cinderella’s clever fairy godmother turned Cinderella’s plain clothes into a glamorous gown, Cinderella’s hair became glossy, and on her feet, were glittery glass slippers.
